About Me

My own professional life in technology began early on - I learned GW-BASIC and fell in love with electronics at the age of 9 simultaneously by experimenting with various kits. Falling in love with electronics as well as computers quite naturally led to my pursuing Electrical Engineering and subsequently Management.

Over the last 20 years, I have been fortunate enough to work with amazing teams and technologies and learn something new every day. My journey took an interesting turn when I was pursuing my master's in Biomedical Signal Processing, where I discovered a wonderful interest in AI/ML. Since then, I've worked with brilliant engineers and leaders who've shaped my technology and team-building style.

What I love is that I really do believe that great software is born of unified, supportive teams. I enjoy bringing people together, instilling a culture in which we can all learn and grow as one, and simplifying complicated things. Whether we're making new products or streamlining existing processes, I believe in empathetic leadership and being curious.
