About Me

My journey in technology began early - at age 9, I discovered GW-BASIC and simultaneously fell in love with electronics while building various kits. This dual passion for tinkering with both computers and electronics naturally led me to pursue Electrical Engineering and later, Management studies.

Over the last 20 years, I've been fortunate to work with amazing teams and technologies, learning something new every day. My path took an exciting turn during my master's studies in Biomedical Signal Processing, where I developed a deep fascination for AI/ML. Since then, I've had the privilege of working alongside talented engineers and leaders who've shaped my approach to technology and team building.

What drives me is the belief that great software comes from collaborative, supportive teams. I enjoy bringing people together, fostering an environment where everyone can learn and grow, and finding simple solutions to complex problems. Whether it's developing new products or improving existing processes, I believe in leading with empathy and staying curious.
